Product Discovery · Competitor Research · Feature Strategy
Found the gap no competitor saw — and turned it into the feature 60% of users chose first
The Situation
In 2018, the US government passed a law mandating e-filing for nonprofit 990 tax forms by 2019. Every player in the market rushed to build an e-filing solution — but all of them followed the same approach: an interview-style filing flow that walked users through questions step by step.
The Discovery
Competitor research revealed that not a single player offered direct form entry — where users fill the form itself, exactly as they would on paper. Nonprofits had been filing 990s on paper for decades. The form was familiar. An interview flow was unnecessary friction. I proposed direct form entry as a parallel filing solution alongside the existing interview style.
The Outcome
60% of users chose direct form entry over the interview style — immediately. Usability and engagement improved significantly. A single insight from competitor research became a unique product differentiator that no competitor offered.

Underserved Market · Product Line Strategy · Free SKU Conversion
Captured 30K small biz users in 2 years by spotting an underserved segment and building the product line they needed
The Situation
Small businesses were an underserved segment in the 1099 & W-2 filing market. They were still filing on paper, bearing huge costs in paper, printing, mailing, and software. Existing providers charged separately for forms and software, making it cost-prohibitive for small businesses.
The Discovery
I recommended a new product line to serve this segment, with a unique twist — provide the forms for free, then upsell e-filing at a low fee. This would be cost-effective for small businesses, eliminate their biggest pain points, and create a compelling reason to switch from paper to e-filing.
The Outcome
The free forms SKU was an instant hit. It captured 25,000–30,000 new small business users within 2 years of launch. The low-cost e-file upsell converted a significant portion of them to paid e-filing. Addressing an underserved segment with a tailored product line drove substantial new user growth and revenue.

Feature Discovery · Competitive Differentiation · TruckLogics
Built the cost estimator no competitor had — now the tool every user checks before a load
The Situation
Trucking companies take on loads without knowing what the trip will actually cost them. Not a single competitor in the fleet management space offered cost estimation for a load or dispatch — every operator was guessing their margins.
The Discovery
We saw this gap as a key differentiator worth building. The solution: identify miles between stops, pull estimated toll charges, calculate fuel costs based on miles, and factor in driver settlements — giving users the complete expected cost of every load before they commit to it.
The Outcome
The feature delivered high usability and adoption immediately. Today, it's the tool every user turns to before taking a load — checking expected costs has become part of their daily workflow. A differentiator no competitor offered became one of the product's most-used features.

Owned the full campaign lifecycle: budgets, campaign types, ad copies, landing pages, and video scripts aligned to each goal. The Outcome

Paid channels consistently contributed 20–30% of conversions, bringing in new users every season. For certain products, search ads returned 200–300% ROI on spend.

Product-Led Growth · Free SKUs · Onboarding Optimisation
Let the product sell itself — while competitors charged subscriptions, we removed every barrier to entry
The Situation
Seasonal products have to be product-led — users show up when they need to file, and every barrier between them and the product costs conversions. Competitors charged subscriptions and forced software downloads before users could even see the value.
The Approach
Leveraged PLG techniques competitors wouldn't — free SKUs, trials, no subscription, no software download, pay only when you're ready to file. Included necessary in-app help — contextual help text, video walkthroughs, support articles with a search bot, and clear actions to take at every step. Ran demos and role plays to pressure-test every screen from the user's point of view.
The Outcome
In TruckLogics, onboarding took users through 10 different steps — causing heavy drop-off. Removed the entire sequence and moved it to settings — onboarding and conversion increased 30–45% across different SKUs. In Tax990, Form 990-N filers were stuck in a complex flow combined with other forms — built a separate 3-step flow letting small nonprofits complete filing in a quick checkout, driving significantly more paid conversions.
